Welcome To Broad Strokes Community Pool

Get Active!

Swimming is an activity that might best be described as "one exercise fits all." It can be practiced by almost anyone, no matter what your age, whether you’re in top shape or recovering from an injury. So, if you’re looking to swim a few laps, do water aerobics, or get a little more extreme and conquer the waterslides, make our beautiful Broad Stokes Community Pool your fitness destination.

Recreational Programs

Do you have an urge to try something new? Our recreational programs may have what you are looking for! Aquatic Sports, Child & Adult Swimming Lessons, Senior Programs, Tennis Programs, and Outdoor Recreational Clubs are only a small sampling of what we have to offer. So come by, sign up, and get fit with us in the pool.  With less stress on the joints of the body, aquatics are an ideal way to get back in shape.  We also offer a variety of weight loss classes including boot camp, aquapump and more.

About Us

Our Broad Strokes Community Pool is situated at 427 York Street in Toronto, Ontario. The facility was built in 2001 and is a member only community club focusing on offering affordable fun that encourages healthy lifestyles for the entire family. The Broad Strokes Community Pool is a hub of activity for families living in the area, and is always staffed with a minimum of two life guards during all hours of operations. Guards are selected after successfully completing a competitive process that occurs in April of each year.
